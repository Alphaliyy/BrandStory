Our Story

We started as a manufacturing factory, focused on one thing: making things faster.

But Bunkie Board changed our thinking.

We tested structure after structure — load distribution, breathability, thickness, resilience, noise control…

All shaping one moment: When you lie down, does your body finally relax?

The turning point came from feedback, not the market.

“My back doesn’t hurt anymore.”

“The bed finally feels stable.”

“Finally, I can sleep well again.”

These words meant more than any data...

“Every rest matters”

We hope you can rest well every day.

May every night bring you the rest you deserve.
